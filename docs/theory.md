## Key Concepts

- **Relational fraud**: Fraud patterns are often network effects (device sharing, merchant abuse).
- **Temporal evaluation**: Random splits leak future behavior; chronological/rolling splits are realistic.
- **Calibration**: Probabilities must match empirical frequencies (ECE, Brier).
- **Conformal prediction**: Provides set-valued predictions with coverage guarantees.
- **Explainability**: SHAP, counterfactuals, and plain-English reports for non-technical stakeholders.
